Introduction

What really brings the world to your fingertips? With infinite troves of knowledge circulating the world wide web, the search bar is often the unsung hero of user interfaces.

From navigating complex websites to discovering new information, the search bar can take you where you want to go, when you want to go there. But have we really mastered its design?

Reflection

Trade-Offs: All components face accessibility trade-offs for different kinds of users. What is convenient for mouse users may not be helpful for keyboard users.

Purpose: It is important to consider the purpose of a component when designing it. For instance, Instagram is a social media platform, so its search bar might focus more on content recommendation than simplicity.
